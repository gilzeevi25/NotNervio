The Need

Monitoring is complex & error‑prone

Intraoperative signals require constant expert interpretation, leaving room for human error.

Only ~20% of surgeries are monitored

A shortage of trained neurophysiologists means many procedures go unmonitored.

Eroding profit margins

High labor costs and inefficiencies threaten the sustainability of monitoring programs.

Our Solution

NotNervio leverages deep‑learning models trained on EMG, SSEP and MEP data to deliver real‑time alerts during surgery.

By converting complex neuro signals into clear guidance, surgeons can respond faster and keep patients safer.
